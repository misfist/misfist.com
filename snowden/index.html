Are donations tax deductible? No. However, we are happy to work with an established 501c3 organization to change this, as long as the funds can be given directly to Edward Snowden.

Edward Snowden might be facing legal consequences as a result of his actions. Could this fundraising effort implicate the organizers or donors in 'aiding and abetting' him? Yes. That's exactly the point, and helps explain why we are raising money directly for Snowden, as opposed to restricting it only for a legal defence fund. This is online civil disobedience. The more of us who participate by donating (even a small amount!) the greater our moral power and ability to protect him from legal consequences.

How can I be in touch with the organizers of this campaign? Email us at people4snowden@gmail.com.

How is this money being collected? We are using the payment gateway for Organizing 2.0, the brand name of Charles Lenchner. He is part of Your Inbox Occupied and has been an Occupy Wall Street activist from the beginning. We may switch the payment gateway in the future.
